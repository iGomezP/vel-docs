# Project Management Platform

## Mission

Provide a structured system to manage engineering work, planning, backlog, research, decisions, and sprint execution inside VEL.

---

## Business Value

The Project Management Platform allows VEL to operate with engineering discipline.

Every important change should be tracked, prioritized, documented, and validated before being considered complete.

---

## Current Implementation

- Plane

---

## Responsibilities

- Backlog management
- Sprint planning
- Work item tracking
- Research tracking
- Documentation tasks
- Engineering evidence
- Roadmap execution

---

## Dependencies

- Plane
- PostgreSQL
- Redis
- RabbitMQ
- MinIO
- Docker

---

## Risks

- Some Plane features may require a paid plan.
- Tool limitations may affect the desired workflow.
- VEL should depend on the process, not the tool.
