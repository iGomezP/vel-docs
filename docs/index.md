# Vakzor Enterprise Lab

Vakzor Enterprise Lab (VEL) is an enterprise engineering platform designed to support deep learning, professional portfolio development, and hands-on experimentation with software architecture, distributed systems, DevOps, observability, security, and artificial intelligence.

## Mission

Design, build, and operate a modern engineering platform that behaves like a real enterprise environment.

## Core principles

- Learn by understanding, not copying.
- Document important decisions.
- Automate when it adds value.
- Prioritize quality over speed.
- Build as if it were production.
- Connect theory with real implementation.
- Keep decisions reversible when possible.
- Treat observability as a first-class concern.
- Leave evidence at the end of every sprint.

## Current phase

**Sprint 0.5 — Engineering Foundation**

The current goal is to establish the management, documentation, and operating standards for VEL before expanding the platform further.
